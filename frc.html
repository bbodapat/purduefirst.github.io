FRC Team 461


FIRST Robotics team 461 is from West Lafayette, Indiana who are passionate about science and technology. Since 1999, technology teacher Steve Florence has encouraged students to pursue their interests in science, engineering, and technology. By working together to accomplish tasks in such fields as technology and public relations, we excite other students and the community about the values of FIRST.

FRC Team 1646


FIRST Team 1646 is from Jefferson High School in Lafayette, Indiana and was founded in 2003. For more than a decade 1646 has been inspiring students and the community. At many events in and out of school they demonstrate their robot and speak about FIRST Robotics.

FRC Team 1747


FRC Team 1747, Harrison Boiler Robotics is a high school FIRST Robotics team comprised of students from Harrison High School in West Lafayette, Indiana. Besides building a robot in six weeks, they are dedicated to spreading the wonders of science and technology throughout their community and they encourage students to become involved in the various FIRST programs.

FRC Team 4272


Robotics Team 4272 Maverick Boiler Robotics had its rookie season in 2012. Created as a partnership between Purdue FIRST Programs and McCutcheon High School, the team serves the population of students at McCutcheon High School in Lafayette, Indiana. 4272 is considered an after school activity and a team by the school, as well as having a class within the school.

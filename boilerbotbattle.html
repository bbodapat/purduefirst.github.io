About

Purdue FIRST Programs is hosting the first ever FRC off season event held at Purdue University: Boiler Bot Battle. We invite FRC teams from across the country to participate in a weekend of robots and college information on Purdue University's main West Lafayette campus. Subscribe to the mailing list with the link above to stay informed as event information is released.

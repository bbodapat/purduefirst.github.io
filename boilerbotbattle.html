About



Be a part of the second Boiler Bot Battle, hosted at Purdue University by Purdue FIRST Programs. Join us at the Purdue Armory on November 10th and 11th for robots, campus tours, social events, and much more. Check back to this page for frequent updates, and we hope to see you in November! Subscribe to the mailing list to stay informed as event information is released.

Aid Opportunities


The Department of Science at Purdue University has made a travel grant available to teams who wouldn't be capable of attending the event due to travel costs. More information and an application to come. here. Email the completed form to gatesh@purdue.edu

- Bowling
- Pool (Billiards)
- Board Games
- A $10 meal ticket for dinner at a Purdue Memorial Union restaurant
